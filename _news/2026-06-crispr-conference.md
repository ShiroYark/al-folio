title: CRISPR 2026 Conference at Yale
---

The [CRISPR 2026 Conference](https://mbb.yale.edu/news/crispr-2026-conference-convenes-yale) was held June 10–12 at Yale University and the Omni New Haven Hotel, bringing together approximately 120 leading scientists from around the world to discuss the latest advances in CRISPR biology — from mechanisms and emerging systems to bacterial defense and genome engineering. I contributed to the organization of the conference alongside Dr. Ailong Ke, Dr. Yan Zhang (University of Michigan), and Dr. Ryan Jackson (Utah State University), and was honored to receive a **Poster Award** for my work on Type IV-C CRISPR system regulation.
